Building Damage Dictionary






The Building Damage Dictionary is a comprehensive guide to various damage types affecting building materials. It helps you assess and diagnose material issues, offers expert repair advice, and expands your technical vocabulary. This app is an indispensable resource for heritage and conservation professionals, architects, builders, educators, students, and homeowners alike.
