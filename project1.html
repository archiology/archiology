Golden Ratio Calculator






The Golden Ratio Calculator is a simple tool that allows you to divide a segment into parts that adhere to the golden ratio. Often called the divine proportion, this mathematical principle has been widely used by designers, architects, photographers, and artists to create harmonious and visually appealing proportions. With this tool, you can quickly determine the golden sum of a given number based on its short, long, or total length.
